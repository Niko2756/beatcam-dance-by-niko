    BeatCam Dance by Niko

    Move with the music

    Use your camera to track body movement, load a song, and dance on the beat. Better timing earns stronger hits, longer combos build multipliers, and missed beats charge a comeback boost for your next Good, Great, or Perfect hit.

    • Click Camera so the game can track you.
    • Enter a player name and choose a song.
    • Wait for Audio map 100%, then press Start.
    • Dance through the countdown and keep your combo alive.
